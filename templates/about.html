About Us

In the era of social media, everybody shares their day-to-day events on platforms like Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, etc., in the form of stories.
Users often upload photos capturing various events such as birthday parties, marriage anniversaries, reunions, etc., accompanied by suitable songs. However, they frequently encounter difficulties in finding the perfect song to complement their photos, leading to the abandonment of the idea of sharing these moments with others.
To address this issue, we have developed a Song Recommendation System. This system aims to detect emotions from images and recommend the most suitable songs for your social media stories.
By integrating this system, users can enhance their social media engagement. While a simple image may convey that you attended an event, adding a carefully selected song allows others to feel and experience the emotions captured in the photo. A song has the power to breathe life into an image, providing a richer and more immersive storytelling experience.
